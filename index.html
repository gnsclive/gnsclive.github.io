About GNSC Live

GNSC Live is the official digital academic broadcasting platform of the Global Neurosurgical Students Council (GNSC). The platform is dedicated to providing accessible, high-quality neurosurgical education to medical students and neurosurgical trainees across the globe.

Our Vision

To unite medical students and neurosurgical trainees worldwide through accessible, equitable, and globally impactful neurosurgical education.

Our Mission

GNSC Live advances the mission of the Global Neurosurgical Students Council by delivering live and recorded neurosurgical educational content under international academic supervision.
