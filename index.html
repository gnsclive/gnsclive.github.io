About GNSC Live

GNSC Live is the official digital academic broadcasting platform of the Global Neurosurgical Students Council (GNSC). We leverage modern technology to bridge the gap in neurosurgical knowledge across continents.

Our Vision

To unite medical students and neurosurgical trainees worldwide through accessible, equitable, and globally impactful neurosurgical education.

Our Mission

GNSC Live advances the mission of the GNSC by delivering live and recorded neurosurgical educational content under international academic supervision.

Events & Live Broadcasts

  • Live neurosurgical lectures and webinars
  • Expert-led panel discussions and workshops
  • Research and mentorship sessions
